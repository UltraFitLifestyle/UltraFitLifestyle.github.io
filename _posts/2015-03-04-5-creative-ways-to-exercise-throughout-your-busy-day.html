Moms are busy these days. Whether you are a stay-at-home mom or a company CEO, we all wish there were a couple more hours in the day to get it all done! Fitting fitness into your schedule may be tricky. Here are five creative ways to fit fitness into your hectic schedule.

Make friends with the stoplight! Instead of getting annoyed on those days when it seems you hit every red light, use those moments to your advantage. For example, practice vacuum breathing at a red light. Place your hands on your belly. Inhale and extend your tummy into your hands. Exhale sharply and pull the abdominals in and imagine them pulling up. This is a great way to train the transverse abdominals and get you one step closer to that coveted six-pack. Love your carpool schedule. Feeling like a one-mom taxi service can get overwhelming. When you are waiting for the kids to finish soccer or gymnastics, use the time to your advantage. Hop out of the car and take a brisk walk. If you have to stay put, start carrying a jump rope in the car. Alternate between 50 skips and 5 pushups. You can even do the pushups standing up by placing your hands on the bumper of your car.
