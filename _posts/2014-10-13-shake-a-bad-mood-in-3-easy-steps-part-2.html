Last blog I wrote was entitled "Shake a Bad Mood in 3 Easy Steps" An amazing blogger pal of mine, named Jessica (check her out HERE!!) read it and let me know she was thinking she was going to get a good Health Shake recipe instead of a bit of advice on how to get rid of the blues. I found that really interesting in two ways:

1) Amazing how different people perceive things.

2) I am a health shake junkie, so that must be a sign I should share my favorite recipe!!

I am a creature of habit; I don’t get too creative with any recipe most of the time. I stick to what works for me. This is mostly because I have analyzed and tweaked my diet so scrupulously due to my Crohn’s disease, in order to discover what my body will and will not tolerate. I rarely stray from a very basic diet routine. When I do, I know that my body may react.

That being said, I DO like yummy things!! I found that nutrition in liquid form makes me feel really good, so I have a shake every day. Usually, I drink it for breakfast. Here’s my recipe:

1 packet Shakeology (if you’d like to know more about what Shakeology is, click HERE)

1 banana

Ice

So Delicious coconut milk – unsweetened

I prefer the Strawberry Shakeology over all the rest, but I like all the flavors and this simple recipe works with them all. My second fav is Chocolate, and I will throw in a TBSP of PB2, which is a killer powdered Peanut Butter!!! I hit the health shake daily; it ALWAYS make me feel fantastic. Think about it: nutrients in liquid form can be absorbed immediately, little to no digestion required. So, your body instantly goes YAY!! try it and you’ll see....
