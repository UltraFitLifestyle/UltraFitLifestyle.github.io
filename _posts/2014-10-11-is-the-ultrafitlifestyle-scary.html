I am passionate about fitness, that’s no surprise. It concerns me that I am hearing lately that I am “scary,” “intense,” and that the UltraFitLifestyle that I talk about all of the time is “unattainable.” The truth is that the UltraFitLifestyle is customized for everyone. Everyone is at a different fitness level, or perhaps even starting from zero. You are not going to have to do what you see ME do. You will start where YOU are capable of starting. It does not have to be scary!

If you go read my story, you can see where I come from and that is why I am so intense with my own fitness. It’s my passion and my profession. I have specific goals. But, I do not expect my clients to follow the schedule I follow. That’s MY schedule that I have worked toward for years. Fitness is also part of how I manage my Crohn’s disease. I enjoy an intense challenge. But, when it comes to your UltraFitLifestyle, I start with YOU.

  • Where are you today?
  • What are your goals?
  • What are your limitations?
  • What are your fears?
  • What do you love to do and what do you hate to do?

I had someone comment on my Facebook page the other day: “I wouldn’t last 5 minutes in your boot camp!” It made me smile, because people must really have the wrong impression about my boot camp. We workout, we sweat, and the girls complain about being sore. But, what we REALLY do for that hour is laugh, talk about our kids and significant others, and help the person who brings a problem “to the table” and needs some help. Sometimes I am the one who needs help with a problem!

So, the UltraFitLifestyle is not really so scary after all. In fact, it could be just exactly what you need. Give it a chance.
